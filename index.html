About

Family-run, pet-centered care with a focus on comfort and communication.

Personal Pet Care and Grooming

At Barks and Bubbles, we’re more than just a pet service - we’re your neighbors in the Burien community. We're a small, family-owned business, and we are dedicated to caring for the animals you love most. Our passion for dogs and cats is backed by years of hands-on experience, so your pet is always in safe, patient, and loving hands.

We are both fully insured and certified in pet first aid, so your pets are cared for by people who are prepared for both the everyday and the unexpected.

Our Approach

  • Low-stress, fear-aware handling: We move at your pet’s pace, using gentle handling and patience, especially for anxious, shy, or senior pets.
  • Customized care for each pet: We tailor grooming, walks, and visits to your pet’s age, coat type, temperament, and daily routine.
  • Clear, honest communication: We keep you updated, and are upfront about timing, pricing, and expectations.
  • Safety and professionalism: We are insured and certified in pet first aid, and we follow careful safety practices in your home and on every walk.

Who We Are

Kacie – Professional Dog Groomer

Kacie is a professional dog groomer with experience working with a wide range of coat types and personalities. She specializes in low-stress, in-home grooming that keeps dogs comfortable while still achieving clean, natural results.

From first-time puppy grooms to seniors and nervous dogs, Kacie focuses on gentle handling, making every appointment feel calm and predictable.

Adam – Pet Care Specialist

Adam is a pet care provider for walks and drop-in visits in your home. He has extensive experience with reactive and big-feelings dogs, and is skilled at reading body language and giving dogs the space and structure they need to feel safe.

Whether it’s a quick potty break, a long walk, or multi-day visits, Adam focuses on consistency, routine, and helping each dog feel understood and supported.
